FAQ
Questions answered.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. All your data is stored locally on your own device. We never see it, sell it, or send it anywhere. There are no analytics trackers built in.
Can I use it on my phone? +
Absolutely. pocket. is a Progressive Web App — open it in your mobile browser and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install it like a regular app. It works offline too.
What's the difference between the plans? +
Free gives you the core tracker. Plus (£5/mo) adds unlimited history and CSV export. Pro (£15/mo) adds multiple pots, family sharing, custom categories, and priority support.
Can I cancel my plan? +
Yes, cancel any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No awkward retention flows.
Does it work for any allowance amount? +
Yes — the allowance, spend caps, and split are all fully adjustable in Settings. Whether you get £10 or £200 a month, pocket. works for you.
